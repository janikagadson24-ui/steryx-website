About SteryX Medical Solutions

SteryX Medical Solutions, LLC is a healthcare solutions company focused on supporting modern healthcare organizations through innovative services, operational support, and healthcare-focused logistics.

The company was founded with the vision of creating a reliable healthcare partner capable of supporting healthcare systems, organizations, and public health initiatives through scalable service solutions.

SteryX is built on the belief that strong operational systems are essential to delivering effective healthcare. By combining healthcare expertise with professional service delivery, the company aims to help healthcare organizations operate more efficiently while maintaining high standards of quality and reliability.

Founder

Janika Taylor, MSN, FNP-BC
Founder & Managing Member

Janika Taylor is a board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner with extensive experience in clinical practice and healthcare operations. With a background spanning urgent care, telehealth, and diverse clinical environments, she brings a deep understanding of healthcare systems and patient-centered care.

Her professional experience includes leadership roles within healthcare organizations, where she has contributed to improving patient care delivery, operational efficiency, and clinical support services.

Through SteryX Medical Solutions, Janika aims to expand innovative healthcare support services that strengthen healthcare organizations and improve access to high-quality healthcare solutions.

Mission

To support and strengthen healthcare systems through innovative, reliable, and scalable healthcare solutions.

Vision

To build a trusted healthcare solutions company that supports healthcare organizations, public health initiatives, and communities through modern service delivery and operational excellence.
